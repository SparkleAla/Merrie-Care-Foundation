ABOUT US

Merrie’s Care is a non-governmental organization, founded by Elizabeth O. Ibisiki a young graduate of Rivers State University, Port Harcourt Nigeria in the year 2019. The foundation is based in the city of Port Harcourt and carries out operations within the city as it is still a growing foundation.

OUR VISION

The vision of Merrie's Care Foundation is to help create a nation where children without parental support can have and access basic social amenities like food, quality education, security and standard health care, beginning from our locale.

Our Mission:

We strife to reach and touch as many lives as we can, catering for at least one hundred children a year with our different programmes and vocational trainings to enable children and young adults learn worthwhile skills that’ll give them a better future.

OUR VALUES

Integrity:

We are committed to following all ethical principles on children welfare to properly carry out our mission under the constraint of the law as regards to children rights and privileges.

Trustworthiness:

Being trustworthy is one of our key values. We will be always be accountable to our patrons and ensure that we manage funds entrusted to us and channel them solely for the wellbeing of the children.

THE PEOPLE OF MERRIE’S CARE

THE FOUNDER – Elizabeth O. Ibisiki

Elizabeth Ibisiki is a graduate of Library and Information Science, Rivers State University Port Harcourt Nigeria. She is the founder and executive director of Merrie’s Care Foundation. Elizabeth is very passionate about child growth and development, being an orphan herself and a very early age, she understands the struggle of not having the basic needs of a growing child.

She started this foundation with a few friends and college mates who share the same vision and are passionate about child care.

"Our future is shaped by our present actions. Children are the future, their wellbeing should be our priority".

Elizabeth O. Ibisiki - Founder Merrie’s Care foundation.
